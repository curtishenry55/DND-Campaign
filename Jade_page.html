Jade, the Emerald Flame

"The soil of Barovia is sour and choked by rot, yet the roots run deep. You feel the forest's heartbeat, though it is erratic—a dying pulse beneath the mists. The Dawn does not reach here, child of green, but the Emerald Flame still burns within you. Let your roots pierce the decay, for even in the shadow of the peaks, the earth remembers those who protect it."

Private Objectives

1. Whisper of the Woods: Follow the voices in the wind. The trees of Barovia are not dead, only sleeping. Their roots remember what the people have forgotten.

2. Starve the Rot: Something unnatural feeds from the land's very life force. Identify the source of the corruption and learn how to sever it.

3. The Broken Cycle: Restore the balance of life and death, even if it demands a sacrifice the mists have yet to name.

4. Ruby's Echo: Your mother’s death was no accident. Track the hand that silenced her voice within this valley.

5. Circle of Shadows: Find the remnants of the Emerald Circle. If any survived the crossing, their fate is bound to your own.

Secrets Only You Know

• When you meditate beneath the moonlight, Ruby's voice warns: “Do not feed the Heart of Sorrow. Each death strengthens him.”

• The light here is tainted; faint red hues dance at the edge of your Moonbeam spells, as if the land itself is bleeding into your magic.

• You awoke with damp soil in your hand—soil that felt alive, breathing, from a silver grove you have only seen in dreams.
