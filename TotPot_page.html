Tot Pot, The Light

Divine Connection: Waning

The Mists Intervene: Your connection to the Dawnfather is desynchronizing. Should this bar fall below 50%, the Light may flicker. Spells may fail, healing may dampen, and the warmth you provide will feel increasingly... cold. Complete objectives and convert others to follow the Dawnfather to strengthen the connection.

"The mists of Barovia do not just block the sun; they swallow the spirit. Even the Dawnfather strains to find you here. You are a beacon, Tot Pot, but every flame needs fuel—and in this valley, the fuel is your own soul."

Sacred Objectives

  • Restore the Dawn’s Voice: Counter the darkness with acts of pure radiance.
  • The Hallowed Ground: Return the Bones of St. Andral to the church in Vallaki.
  • The Great Commission: Convert 3 non-believers to the Light before the mist takes them.
  • Divine Record: Witness and document a true Miracle performed in His name.
  • The Shoemaker’s Fate: Assist the old woman at the church with her desperate plea.

Secrets of the Soul

  • The Debt: Channelling the Light feels less like a gift and more like a transaction. What are you trading for this power?
