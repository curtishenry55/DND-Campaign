Thorgar, the Mountain

"The foundations of Barovia still tremble with the memory of giants and the echo of the avalanche. You walk where the mountains once stood tall—now, you are the only peak left in this sunless valley. When the thunder answers your fury, do not just strike; listen. The storm calls you by your true name—the one you tried to bury beneath the corpses of your enemies. Feed the storm your courage, Thorgar, for if you feed it only your wrath, it will consume the mountain whole."

Main Character Objectives

  • Prove your strength without succumbing to the rage; Barovia tests more than muscle, it tests the man behind it.
  • Hunt the beast that stalks the perimeter of your dreams—the truth is hidden in its hide.
  • Protect Jade when the earth begins to tremble, regardless of the blood price.
  • Seek a tribute worthy of the peaks; reclaim the lost strength of the mountains.
  • Satiate Cravenedge... or learn to master the hunger before it masters you.
  • Persuade Jade to... "unsheath your sword."

Secrets Only You Know

  • The Darkest Impulse: In the dead of night at the camp, you felt the cold steel of your blade urging you to slaughter the sleeping children. You barely held back.
  • The Next Scent: The blade is restless. If it is not fed soon, you fear you may not get to choose its next target.
  • A Soft Spot: You will miss the boy, Freak. His admiration is a rare thing in this land. Perhaps a parting gift is warranted before the orphanage gates close.
