Thorgar, the Mountain

Cravenedge Hunger

The blade pulses against your palm. It is restless. If the red does not reach the tip soon, it may find its own way to feed.

"The foundations of Barovia still tremble with the memory of giants. You walk where the mountains once stood tall—now, you are the only peak left. When the thunder answers your fury, listen. The storm calls you by your true name. Feed the storm your courage, Thorgar, for if you feed it only your wrath, it will consume the mountain whole."

Main Character Objectives

  • Restraint: Prove your strength without succumbing to the rage.
  • The Beast: Hunt the creature that stalks your dreams; the truth is in its hide.
  • The Vow: Protect Jade when the earth begins to tremble, no matter the blood price.
  • Legacy: Seek a tribute worthy of the peaks; reclaim the lost strength of the mountains.
  • The Master: Satiate Cravenedge... or learn to master the hunger before it masters you.
  • Bond: Persuade Jade to... "unsheath your sword."

Secrets Only You Know

  • The Impulse: At camp, you felt the cold steel urging you to slaughter the sleeping children.
  • The Hunger: If the blade isn't fed soon, you fear you may not get to choose the next victim.
  • The Gift: You will miss the boy, Freak. A parting gift might be appropriate before leaving Vallaki.
